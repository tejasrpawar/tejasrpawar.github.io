About

Hello! I’m Tejas, a passionate and driven graduate student specializing in Computer Science, currently based in Salt Lake City, Utah. I am pursuing my MS degree in Computer Science from the University of Utah. With a strong background in software engineering, I have a proven track record of delivering high-quality software solutions and optimizing technology to meet business goals.

Before getting into Graduate school, I worked as Full-Stack Software Engineer at Airtel Digital, India for 2 years where I worked on multiple platforms ranging from E-commerce, Payments Bank in an Agile environment following latest industry practices like Microservice architecture, Frontend frameworks, Data streaming and DevOps CI/CD pipelining to name a few.

While I was an Undergraduate student I interned at the same firm as a Software Engineer for Six months, working on building microservices from scratch and engineering and optimising RESTful APIs for traffic distribution and management. I also designed and implemented logging solutions which reduced the system downtime.

I completed my undergraduate studies majoring in Information Technology from The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad (IIITA). During my time there, notably, I worked on High Perfomance Computing(Surya) and was a part of the University Competitive Programming club. I was also a Senator in the Students Body of the institute.

My areas of Expertise: Software Engineering, Machine Learning, Backend Engineering/Developement, Spring Boot, Microservice Architecture, Data Streaming(Apache Kafka, Aerospike), Database Management.
